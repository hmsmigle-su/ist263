OttoTHON

Syracuse University's Dance Marathon

OttoTHON is Syracuse University's 12 hour Dance Marathon. The organization fundraises money for the Children at Upstate Golisano Children's Hospital. The money raised goes towards helping kids and families battling pedicatric ilness and are treated at Upstate. Last year, OttoTHON 2019 rasied $202,428.56 do help build the Upstate Center for Special Needs.
