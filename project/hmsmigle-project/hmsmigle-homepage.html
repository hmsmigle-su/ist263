What is OttoTHON?

OttoTHON is Syracuse University's 12 hour Dance Marathon. The organization fundraises money for the Children at Upstate Golisano Children's Hospital. All of the money raised goes towards helping kids and families battling pedicatric illness and are treated at Upstate. Since it's founding in 2014, the organization has rasied over half a million dollars and has become the University's largest student run philanthropy! Last year, OttoTHON 2019 rasied $202,428.56 to help build the Upstate Center for Special Needs.
