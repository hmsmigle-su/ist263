Children's Mircale Network Hospitals

Children's Miracle Network Hospitals is a non-profit organization that rasies money for Children's Hospitals across America. They promote the education of children's health issues and medical research. Not every hospital is a CMN Hospital, but lucky enough, Upstate Golisano is one of the hundreds of CMN Hospitals. Dance Marathon events are put on by colleges all across the country and a lot of them are partenered with a Children's Miracle Network.
