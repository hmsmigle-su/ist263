Children's Mircale Network Hospitals

Children's Miracle Network Hospitals is a non-profit organization that rasies money for Children's Hospitals across America. They promote the education of children's health issues and medical research. Not every hospital is a CMN Hospital, but lucky enough, Upstate Golisano is one of the hundreds of CMN Hospitals. Dance Marathon events are put on by colleges all across the country and a lot of them are partenered with a Children's Miracle Network. Want to find your local Children's Miracle Network Hosptial? With the current COVID-19 pandemic sweeping acorss the globe, it is improtant to continue to support children's health. The Children's Hospitals COVID-19 fund has been implemented to raise money to purhase the proper resources for some of the most vulnerable patients to keep them away from the virus. History of Dance Marathon

Miracle Network was founded in 1983 and raised money for 22 hospitals across the United States. The organization was able to raise $4.8 million in its first year! In 1984, 13 year old Ryan White recieved a bad blood transfusion and was diagnosed with HIV/AIDS. The summer going into his freshman year at Indiana University, Ryan White passed away. In his memory, his friends put toghether the first college dance marathon at Indiana University. This was the begining of dance marathon partenering with Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. As years passed more and more universities joined the movement, raising money for their local Children's Miracle Network Hospital.
